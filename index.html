SkillForge AI
Career Intelligence Platform

Problem: Job seekers often don't know exactly which skills are missing from their profile for the roles they want.

Built: A full-stack platform that ingests LinkedIn job postings, extracts skill entities using a fine-tuned Transformer pipeline (JobBERT + Sentence-BERT), scores resumes against job requirements, and delivers gap analysis via an LLM chatbot powered by Groq.

03
AI Search · Simulation · Feb–Mar 2026

Disaster Evacuation
AI Simulation

Problem: Emergency evacuation routing is a life-or-death optimization problem where sub-optimal paths have real consequences.

Built: Modeled city evacuations as a search problem and implemented A* with three heuristics — Misplaced Tiles, Manhattan Distance, and Linear Conflict — to compute provably optimal escape routes. A React frontend visualizes the path-finding step by step, with a Flask backend handling the search computations.

Why it matters: Demonstrates solid CS fundamentals — algorithm design, heuristic analysis, and real-time visualization — packaged into an educational interactive tool.

⚡ A* with 3 heuristics · Optimal path guarantees · Step-by-step visualization
React 18FlaskPythonA* SearchCSS3Vercel

Computer Vision · Dec 2025–Jan 2026

Potato Leaf
Disease Classifier

Problem: Early and Late Blight are major threats to potato crops, and visual diagnosis is slow and error-prone for farmers.

Built: A CNN image classifier trained on leaf images to detect both disease types. Evaluated rigorously with confusion matrices and per-class metrics, then deployed as a real-time Streamlit app where anyone can upload a leaf photo and get an instant diagnosis.

Why it matters: Applied AI to agriculture — a domain where accurate, fast tools have direct economic impact.

⚡ ~96% validation accuracy · Deployed Streamlit app · Full evaluation suite
PythonTensorFlow/KerasOpenCVNumPyStreamlit
